Vision

Imbuing MIT-style learning

MIT Engineering Innovation Competition: Africa is an initiative aimed at raising the technical experience level of engineering undergraduates in African universities.

Our mission is to provide African university students with the tools to not only enhance the skills they have acquired in the classroom but also to become an integral and efficient part of solving major issues globally and locally in their neighboring communities.

This competition is designed to pose a relevant and open ended question, allowing students to come up with their own original, creative and innovative solutions in a technologically progressive context. Hence we are targeting problem solving minds and those who seek to tackle big challenges.
